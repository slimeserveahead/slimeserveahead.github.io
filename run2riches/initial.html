Frequently Asked Questions
How to use Run2Riches?
First, you need to own a pair of shoes and participate in the walking challenge to count your steps and receive rewards. To ensure that each account is genuine and maintain a high-quality community, you need to verify your real name first. After completing the verification, Run2Riches will give you the first pair of shoes for FREE. By completing 800 steps of walking each day, you will receive 5 WCoin instantly. Click on the "Claim Reward" section to not miss out on your rewards.
How do I withdraw the money I earned on Run2Riches?
The WCoin earned through completing walking tasks can be converted into VND (Vietnamese Dong) and easily withdrawn to your bank account.
The minimum withdrawal amount is 50,000 VND.
Step 1: Go to the "Trading Platform" section.
Step 2: Click on "Withdraw" or "Cash Out."
Step 3: Choose the linked bank.
Step 4: Enter the amount and fill in your personal information completely.
Step 5: Confirm and perform "Face ID Scan."
Note: The transaction is expected to be completed within a timeframe of 3 to 5 minutes and is supported 24/7.
Can Run2Riches be used on a computer?
Please note that this app is specifically designed for mobile phones and cannot be used on a computer. If you want to use the app to track fitness and health data, please ensure that you have installed the app on your personal mobile phone. If you need a similar app on your computer, you can try searching for other available software and make sure they are compatible with the operating system of your computer.
How many levels does the Run2Riches shoes have?
Currently, there are 6 types of shoes, equivalent to 6 levels (LV0 - LV5). The first pair of shoes is given for FREE, and the other pairs of shoes correspond to different levels. You can use WCoin to purchase them or receive them for free by "Checking in to get rewards."
What does the 'Store' in Run2Riches have?
In the "Store," there are promising rewards, and WCoin is the key to the lucky draw program, bringing attractive rewards. Place your bet now and get ready to experience the exciting results that await you!
What rewards will there be when introducing friends?
When you introduce friends to use the app, after they register an account and verify their real name with your referral code and become your direct referrals, your contribution value will increase. The more direct referrals you have, the higher your contribution value will be, and your level will also increase. If your direct referrals have shoes, your activity will also increase, thus increasing your daily income and reducing transaction fees.
In Run2Riches, are transactions guaranteed to be safe?
Run2Riches has implemented various measures to ensure the safety of transactions. The platform uses encryption technology to protect users' personal information and transaction data, and employs multi-factor authentication methods to ensure that only authorized individuals can access their accounts and wallets. Additionally, the app verifies the user's submitted identification information to ensure that only verified users can engage in transactions. However, any online transaction carries certain risks, so users should exercise caution when using the platform for transactions and refrain from disclosing their account and password information indiscriminately.
